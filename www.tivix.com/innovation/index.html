Innovation

Tivix combines rock-solid engineering expertise with battle-tested process for creating innovation. We've helped clients to develop innovative new products in the financial services and real estate sectors; we've helped billion dollar companies develop new software for internal efficiencies; we've helped the World Food Program and the European Union to build new software applications for facilitating social benefit.

  • Kickoff Sprints

    We kick off every new engagement with a Tivix Workshopbased on the Google Ventures Product Design Sprint. By getting everyone from the client side and the Tivix side in one room for a kickoff workshop, we shortcut usual debate cycles and get rapid clarity and consensus on what we are building – and why.

  • Rapid Iteration

    We typically emerge from the initial phase of an engagement with proof-of-concept prototype, so that real-world feedback can be incorporated immediately. We then continue with an iterative process throughout the engagement, with rapid release cycles and agile methodology.

  • Entrepreneurial Mindset

    Effective innovation requires a certain entrepreneurial mindset: the agility of a small team; the ability to thrive in the fast-paced world of design sprints and engineering sprints; the ability to understand the business drivers behind a product while thinking of innovative ways to support those drivers and engage users. Tivix brings that mindset to every client engagement.

Our expertise

Django/Python

We believe passionately in the power of Django-Python for rapid development of scalable software applications. We've been a Django-Python shop since 2008. See our code at github.com/tivix

Mobile

The Tivix team develops native iOS and Android applications (Objective-C, Swift, and Java), typically connected via cloud-based API's. Mobile experiences are driving user expectations today, and Tivix has deep experience in building mobile applications that solve real-world problems.

Cloud

Tivix is well-steeped in the field of modern cloud computing infrastructure, both public and private. Many of the applications we develop benefit from the instant-scalability of cloud infrastructure, and all benefit from exposing shared services via well-structured API's for mobile and other applications.
