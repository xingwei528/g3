Mateusz Sikora

Technical Architect

Mateusz earned a Master of Computer Science at the Wrocław University of Technology, Poland in 2009. His master's thesis focused on solving the TSP (Traveling Salesman Problem) with a neural network (Hopfield network) and emphasizing the selection of its parameters using a genetic algorithm.

He began commercial programming as a software engineer in 2007 at AVServis, the leader in Service Center Systems development in Eastern Europe. Mateusz spent the next stage of his career developing several web applications including Modasphere, Surebilling, and clubare, providing him with experience in a range of environments such as Python, Django, and Pylons.

To relax, Mateusz enjoys books and the cinema. He is an organizer for the KAN Amateur and Independent Film Festival in Wrocław, Poland as well as playing basketball and snowboarding in the winter satisfy his love of sports.
