Peter Shafer

Technical Architect

Peter began teaching himself about web development technologies as a kid. He then went on to graduate from Edinboro University of Pennsylvania with a BS in Computer Science and never looked back. Today he's a multi-disciplinary engineer with experience ranging from project management to hard core coding.

While he's a full-stack developer (Django/Python/CSS/JavaScript) he is particularly enthusiastic about modern frameworks such as Angular.

In his spare time, you can find him playing roller hockey, writing about video games, and walking his pug, Watson.
