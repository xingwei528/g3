Mike Gilroy

Software Engineeer

Mike studied University of Birmingham in the UK where he earned his BSc (Hons) in Economics. During his time at University he went to work as an iOS specialist at an Apple Store, where he fell in love with the iOS ecosystem and has since amassed over 500 apps on his iPhone alone. 


Over the past 3 years Mike has gained extensive experience in paid app and technology advertising through advertising APIs such as Facebook, Twitter, Instagram and Apple iAds, whilst working for a leading London media agency. In 2015, Mike went all-in with his passion for iOS development and attended DevMountain, an industry-leading code school nestled in the heart of the Wasatch Mountains of Utah. Here he immersed himself in Objective-C, Swift and all things iOS, as well shipping his first iOS app, QuidClaim (http://www.quidclaim.com). 


Mike grew up in the heart of the British countryside but has lived in London the past few years and absolutely loves the city life and London pubs. He also dreams of one day achieving his YachtMaster qualification, buying a boat and sailing the world.
