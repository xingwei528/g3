Adam Lord

Technical Architect

Adam came to Tivix after passing through the worlds of politics and nonprofits (he was Deputy Director of Online Communications for the Democratic National Convention in 2008).

He has a strong eye for design and UI, loves writing clean markup and efficient CSS, and likes to view web development as a series of puzzles to solve. Today, he is one of our leading experts in responsive design and UI frameworks. He values simplicity, crisp lines, and elegant communication. He holds a degree from Duke University, and in his spare time, Adam likes to cook, play music, and fight the power.
