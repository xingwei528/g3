Maxim Kukhtenkov

Software Engineer

Maxim completed his Master's Degree in Computer Engineering and then boarded a plane the very next day to start his new job at Tivix.

Prior to his Master's at Manhattan College he received a BS in Civil Engineering from the same school, where he graduated magna cum laude.

During his academic tenure he accumulated a variety of honors including Tau Beta Pi, Chi Epsilon, was on the Dean's list, and is a member of the Institute of Electronics Engineers (IEEE).

Comfortable with a range of technologies and frameworks, from Java to C++, he is enjoying immersing himself in developing Django/Python applications at Tivix.

When not writing code, you can find Maxim exploring different places in New York or finding interesting parks and hiking spots outside of the city.
