Will Liu

Software Engineer

Will graduated Magna Cum Laude with a degree in computer engineering technology from DeVry University, learning how to program microcontrollers in assembly language. 

Armed with a new degree and idealistic aspirations, he promptly joined the Peace Corps, which led him to the Kingdom of Tonga in the South Pacific.

Over the years he continued working for a variety of nonprofits creating data visualizations and doing statistical data analysis with SPSS, R, and Python’s Pandas & SciPy.

He entered machine learning competitions using scikit-learn to analyze various datasets and found online user interaction patterns interesting.

By combining his ability to build complex software with analysis of data generated from his applications, Will has become a specialist in creating data-driven web applications.

In his spare time, he likes to read, hike, and play games (mainly Kerbal Space Program).
