Mariusz Karwala

Software Engineer

Mariusz was a PHP developer for several years before discovering Python. He was amazed by the beauty and power of Python and it got him thinking: "Hey... maybe I can use it for building web applications?"

Then he discovered Django, and his life was changed.

He decided that he wanted to extend his Django knowledge and he knew that working with the best people was the way to do that. So he decided to join the great team of Django professionals at Tivix.

In his free time Mariusz enjoys good comedy, documentary movies, and discovering every nook and cranny of Wroclaw.
