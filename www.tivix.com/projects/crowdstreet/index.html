CrowdStreet

Real Estate Crowdfunding

The lead investor in CrowdStreet, Seven Peaks Ventures, referred the startup to Tivix after raising their initial round of funding. 


We conducted a 3-day Tivix product sprint workshop with the executive team to fully understand the business model, positioning, and go-to-market plan. We then built the innovative platform from the ground-up, designed to apply the crowdfunding model to real estate investments, lowering transaction friction in innovative ways to disrupt a well-established sector. Once launched, we transitioned ongoing support and development to the internal engineering team.  

Crowdstreet today remains one of the fastest-growing startups in the sector, using the Tivix-built platform to connect accredited investors with institutional-grade real estate investments in a way that is changing the way business is done.Read about a recent $18.4 million equity fund placed on the Crowdstreet platform.

Technologies used: Django, Python, JavaScript, PostgreSQL.
