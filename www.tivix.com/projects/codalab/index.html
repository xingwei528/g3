CodaLab is a web-based platform that enables researchers, developers, and data scientists to collaborate on data-intensive and computation-intensive projects.  The goal is to advancing Machine Learning and Advanced Computation research in private industry as well as academia. 

Tivix was retained by the core members of the consortium including faculty members at Stanford and UC Berkeley, along with Microsoft Research. We jumped on a plane to meet with the team, immersed ourselves in the project, and are continuing to develop a powerful platform to be used by researchers and academics around the world.
