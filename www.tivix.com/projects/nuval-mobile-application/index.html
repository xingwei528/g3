Tivix listened closely to our business needs and developed an iOS app that has changed the way our core business operates.

Anne Berrnier, Senior Director, NuVal
Nuval, an associated company of Topco, retained Tivix to develop a new mobile application to improve internal operations. Located in Quincy, Massachusetts , NuVal licenses its proprietary food scoring system to food retailers across the country.


Tivix met with the senior executives at the company to understand their current workflow and underlying business model. We then developed iOS and Android native applications, connected to a cloud server via documented API's. 

For Android we used the latest Google design patterns in Material Design. Simple design, intuitive navigation and powerful features were the goals that guide our development process. 

Technologies used: Objective-C (iOS) and Java (Android) Retrofit, ORMLite, ZXing.
