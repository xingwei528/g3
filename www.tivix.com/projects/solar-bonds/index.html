Having worked with Tivix on several projects, I’ve found their unique combination of hard-core engineering skills and solid business acumen to be a phenomenal asset.

John Witchel, Senior Technical Architect, SolarCity
Tivix developed an innovative new financial services for startup Common Assets, which was then acquired by SolarCity, and Tivix continues to support and expand the platform.


Now branded as Solar Bonds, and the platform is revolutionizing the way in which renewable energy projects are financed by opening up bonds to individual investors. The initial $200 million in bonds have already been placed on the platform, and SolarCity has lined up $3 billion worth of contract payments over the next 20 years.

With a very successful IPO in 2012, SolarCity is one of the most innovative companies in the world today. With Elon Musk (PayPal, SpaceX, Tesla) as Chairman , and John Witchel (co-founder of Prosper) as Technical Architect , Solar City is truly an all-star team.

Technologies used: Django, Python, Javascript.
